About Me

Nwali Stanley, known as The Gen Z Voice, is a rising spoken word artist from Ebonyi State, Nigeria, using words to spark light in a world that often feels dark. His journey began quietly—recording himself in videos and audio, performing in mirrors, and speaking courage into empty rooms when no one was watching. Every whisper of effort was a rehearsal for the purpose now unfolding.

Fueled by a deep love for writing and a passion for expression, Stanley grew from self-presentations into a voice for a generation. His mission? To liberate hearts and minds through the power of spoken word. With every verse, he seeks to prove that with voice, Gen Z can rise.
